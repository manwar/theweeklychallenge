HEADLINES

Laurent Rosenfeld shared a very special blog where he revisited his work on the Collatz Sequence task of the Perl Weekly Challenge - 054. He also mentioned in his blog about an interesting discussion among the Perl Mongers.

I noticed, Roger Bell_West is on the loose. He has been getting the blogs of past challenge done. Last week we had blogs for Perl Weekly Challenge - 052 and Perl Weekly Challenge - 053.

A newly joined member, Jared Martin, also shared blogs Task #1 and Task #2 of the Perl Weekly Challenge - 055.

Matthew Somerville joined last week, shared his first blog about his contributions.

I would also like to mention another blog by Cheok-Yin Fung. I found it very informative.
