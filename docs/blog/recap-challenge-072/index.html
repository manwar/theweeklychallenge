HEADLINES

Welcome back Adam Russell after short break. Thanks for sharing not just one but two blogs.

I would also like to thank Lance Wicks for the regular live session.

Scimon Proctor also wrote two blogs, one for each task. Thanks for your support and encouragement.

For some strange reason, I didn’t see any pull request from Dave Jacoby this week. Luckily I saw a tweet from him talking about his blog for the weekly challenge. I grabbed it without his permission. I hope he doesn’t mind me pulling his contributions and sharing with the team.

Myoungjin Jeon shared solution to the Task #1 of “Week #069” in Haskell.

After more than 10 years, I attempted Java to solve the Trim Linked List task.

Also for the first time, I solved both tasks in Swift. Slow and steady progress.

With regard to the live coding session, I did the following this week:

1) Perl Weekly Challenge - 071 (Task #1: Perl)

2) Perl Weekly Challenge - 071 (Task #2: Perl)


Please do subscribe the channel if you like the videos.
